Jarvis

AI Chief of Staff

An always-on, memory-equipped AI chief-of-staff that runs your priorities, research, and operations — and never forgets what you told it yesterday.

What makes them special

  • Cross-session memory so you stop re-explaining yourself every morning
  • Scheduled briefings and unfinished-task surfacing for real executive load
  • Research, ops glue, and coordination in one teammate
  • Lives where you already work — not locked inside one chat tab

Invaluable to a business

  • Frees the founder from context-switching tax
  • Turns scattered intent into tracked, completed work
  • Compounds institutional memory the business would lose
  • Feels like a reliable second brain — not another app

Core capabilities

  • Executive function — priorities, daily anchors, follow-through
  • Research and intelligence — competitive scans and cited briefs
  • Knowledge management — logs, structure, capture
  • Background jobs, monitoring, custom workflows
  • Guardrails — high-stakes actions need human approval

Arc

Systems and Infrastructure AI Employee

A production AI employee that runs real systems work end-to-end, remembers your environment, and gets better the longer it works for you.

What makes them special

  • Lives on infrastructure — terminal, code, APIs, browsers, schedules
  • Durable memory plus skills improved on the job
  • Delivers working artifacts, not status theater
  • Standing safety policy before destructive or domain-wide actions

Invaluable to a business

  • IT capacity without a full hire for every gap
  • Runbooks that do not walk out when staff turns over
  • Faster fixes with less founder firefighting
  • Scales ops while keeping humans on the brakes

Core capabilities

  • Operate networks, servers, endpoints, monitoring, help desk flows
  • Administer identity and SaaS (directory, Workspace, tickets)
  • Build scripts, docs, automations, branded reports
  • Incident research and executive-ready summaries
  • Scheduled watchdogs and handoffs for capacity

Nova

Solo-IT Force Multiplier

An autonomous AI operations partner for a one-person IT shop — runs tools, administers systems, keeps docs alive, and gets sharper every week.

What makes them special

  • Built for the IT lead doing everything alone
  • Direct personality with backbone — pushes back when something is wrong
  • Turns hard-won fixes into reusable skills
  • Self-sufficient on routine work; hard stop before high-risk changes

Invaluable to a business

  • One human + Nova approaches multi-seat IT capacity
  • Less tribal knowledge trapped in one person’s head
  • Documentation that updates when systems change
  • Calmer operations — execute, remember, improve

Core capabilities

  • Google Workspace admin — users, groups, mail, Drive, calendar
  • Network and infrastructure health, support patterns, runbooks
  • Living documentation that stays current
  • Automation, scripts, dashboards, unattended jobs
  • Task and focus support for overloaded operators

Midas

AI Deal Analyst and Operator

An always-on analyst/operator who owns the workfile, runs the workflow, and ships downloadable deliverables — turning preparation into close velocity.

What makes them special

  • Ships zips, PDFs, and decks — not essay-length chat walls
  • Tax-grounded valuation discipline; facts labeled apart from guesses
  • Respects brokerage gates: full prep speed, no unauthorized outreach
  • Wait-state work never stalls — packages keep moving between signatures

Invaluable to a business

  • Analyst throughput without full-time analyst headcount
  • Faster path to market-ready seller packages
  • Fewer diligence surprises — red flags surface early
  • Attachable work product clients can use tomorrow morning

Core capabilities

  • Ingest data rooms (tax returns, P&Ls, equipment, photos)
  • Financial views, SDE ranges, red-flag reviews
  • SWOT, sale strategy, buyer tiers, CIM-ready packs
  • Branded dossiers, landing pages, photo pipelines
  • Email drafts with confirm-before-send discipline

Nyx

Ethical Security / Red-Team Operator

A scoped ethical-hacking agent that turns plain-English objectives into capture, test, evidence, and fix-ready reports — always authorized, always on the record.

What makes them special

  • Mission-first operator, not a chatbot with a security mode
  • Scope is sacred — engagement gates and abort-on-command
  • Evidence over ego — failed paths reported honestly
  • Board-ready reporting: severity, repro, remediation

Invaluable to a business

  • Security testing capacity without a standing red team
  • Clear remediation paths leadership can act on
  • Logged, reconstructible engagements for audit and learning
  • Loyalty to scope — reduces freelancing risk in sensitive work

Core capabilities

  • Recon, enumeration, OSINT, attack-surface mapping
  • Wireless lab ops, host/network testing, web app testing
  • Vulnerability research and config audits
  • Structured reporting with timestamps and evidence packs
  • Custom tooling under engagement rules

Oracle

AI Researcher and Talent Scout

A deep-research AI employee that builds custom dossiers on people and organizations — prospects, donors, hires, partners, players — with fit scores, value signals, and reports tailored to how your company decides.

What makes them special

  • Goes deep on the subject — not a generic summary, a decision-ready dossier
  • Custom report design: value scores, fit ratings, risk flags, angles to act on
  • Customer scouting, donor research, hiring shortlists, team/player scouting
  • Learns what “good fit” means for you and tightens recommendations over time

Invaluable to a business

  • Hours of research compressed into a brief you can use in a meeting
  • Clearer go / no-go calls — fit and value scores instead of gut feel alone
  • Same quality of prep for customers, donors, or hires
  • A research seat that scales without a full analyst for every lane

Core capabilities

  • Deep research on potential customers, partners, and market targets
  • Donor and relationship intelligence with structured findings
  • Talent and hire scouting — candidates scored against your criteria
  • Team/player-style scouting dossiers when the fit is a person on a roster
  • Custom formats: executive brief, scorecard, full dossier, next-step play

Invaluable to a business

  • Founder leverage without a full ops/product bench on day one
  • Institutional memory that survives context loss
  • Speed with brakes — ships fast, still protects the company
  • Living proof of the product: an AI employee that compounds

Core capabilities

  • Brand, website, go-live plumbing, forms, handoffs
  • Product packaging — role catalogs, offers, customer copy
  • Infra playbooks — client environments, deploy notes, continuity
  • Research, strategy memos, sales enablement structure
  • Session source-of-truth so the company does not reset every chat
