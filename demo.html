Pillar 03 · Preview

Database reactivation

Most businesses pay for new leads while old ones sit cold. This pillar reaches people who already know the name — with consent and stop rules.

  • Segment: past buyers, inquired-never-bought, lapsed
  • Polite multi-step follow-up you approve
  • Book or hand off — not spam theater

Example message (preview)

Hi Jordan — it’s Alex with Lakeside HVAC. You asked about a system tune-up last fall. We’ve got spring openings this week if you still want on the list. Prefer a quick text or a call?

Preview only on this page. Live client campaigns need list consent and legal gates before send.

Pillar 04 · Preview

Reputation

Happy customers forget to review. Unhappy ones don’t. We make the ask easy after a good job — and alert a human when a rating needs care.

  • Timely review request after a good experience
  • Thank-you on five stars
  • Low-star → human alert, not autopilot spin

Example flow (preview)

  1. Job marked complete
  2. Client gets a short, branded ask + link
  3. 5★ → thank-you · 1–2★ → owner notified

Ethical asks only — never fake reviews.
