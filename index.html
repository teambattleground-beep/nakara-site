The problem

  1. Hiring is slow, expensive, and hard to reverse.

    You need capacity now. A W-2 hire takes months and still needs training, management, and benefits — for work that may not fill a full role.

  2. AI tools still leave you as the operator.

    Chatbots, automations, and copilots help — until someone has to wire them, watch them, and clean up after them. The stack grows. The bottleneck doesn’t leave.

  3. You wanted a teammate. You got another app.

    What most businesses actually need is person-shaped help: research done, drafts ready, follow-ups sent, tickets triaged — with a human in the loop when it matters.

What you stop doing
