Our posture

Nakara is built for real business operations. We design AI teammates with clear limits, human escalation, and least-privilege access to the tools a role actually needs.

Access

Client systems and credentials are shared only as needed for the engagement. Founder and operator access is limited; secrets are not pasted into public channels.

Data handling

We prefer durable vaults and controlled hosts over ad-hoc copies. Public chat and demo lines are scoped to Nakara’s own business unless a separate client deployment is live.

Incidents
