How we engage

Simple path. No 40-feature tour.

  1. Discover — what’s draining you, what “good” looks like
  2. Design — the smallest stack that solves it
  3. Deploy — pilot on real work, with clear ownership
  4. Improve — tune weekly; expand only when it’s earning keep

High-stakes decisions stay human. We design escalation in from day one — calm operations, not black-box automation.
